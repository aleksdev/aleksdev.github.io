HexaMania 2

Description

HexaMania 2 is a free puzzle game available on Android and iOS.

It features five interesting puzzle games in one. Despite the simplicity of the rules, each mode is very challenging, so everyone can find something interesting for themselves.

INHEX mode:

Place shapes on the game field and create groups of four or more tiles with the same color. These groups of tiles will be cleared, and you'll receive score points. Rotate shapes to find the best position. You can store one shape for later use and you can undo your last move. Clear the tiles to get bombs. Place the bomb on any occupied cell to clear it. The game continues as long as you can place shapes, and your goal is to get as many points as you can.

IHEX GRAVITY mode:

Place shapes on the game field and watch as they fall to the bottom of the game field. Create groups of four or more tiles with the same color to clear them and receive score points. In this mode, you can't rotate the shapes. You can store one shape for later use and you can undo your last move. Clear the tiles to get bombs, and place them on any occupied cell to clear it. The game continues as long as you can place shapes, and your goal is to get as many points as you can.

RINGS mode:

Place shapes on the game field and create rings of the same color. These rings of tiles will be cleared, and you'll receive score points. Rotate shapes to find the best position. You can store one shape for later use and you can undo your last move. Clear the rings to get multicolor shapes and bombs. Place the bomb on the center of any ring to clear the tiles around it. The game continues as long as you can place shapes, and your goal is to get as many points as you can.

MERGED mode:

Place shapes on the game field and create groups of three or more tiles with the same number. These groups of tiles will be merged into the tile with the next number. The maximum tile number is 8, and tiles with number 8 will merge into multicolor tiles. An explosion will occur at the place where the multicolor tiles merge and clear all the tiles around. Rotate shapes to find the best position. You can store one shape for later use and undo your last move. Clear the tiles to get bombs, and place them on any occupied cell to clear it. The game continues as long as you can place shapes, and your goal is to get as many points as you can.

MERGED GRAVITY mode:

Place shapes on the game field and watch as they fall to the bottom of the game field. Create groups of four or more tiles with the same number to merge them into the tile with the next number. The maximum tile number is 9, and tiles with number 9 will merge into multicolor tiles. An explosion will occur at the place where the multicolor tiles merge and clear all cells on the same row. Rotate shapes to find the best position. You can store one shape for later use and you can undo your last move. Clear the tiles to get bombs, and place them on any occupied cell to clear it. The game continues as long as you can place shapes, and your goal is to get as many points as you can.
