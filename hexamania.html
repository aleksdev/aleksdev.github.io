HexaMania Puzzle

Description

Hexa Mania Puzzle is a free puzzle game for Android.

Six interesting puzzle games in one. Despite of rules simplicity every mode is very challenging! Everyone can find something interesting for himself. Sign in to Google Play Games to compare your scores with scores of your friends and players from around the world.

Mode 777

Place figures to game field to make lines which will be cleared and you get score points. You can undo last move. Game continues until you able to place at least one figure. Try to get as much points as you can.

Mode 777+

This mode very similar to 777 mode with additional figures and ability to rotate them.

Mode Get 12

Tap on adjacent tiles with same number to select them and combine to one tile with next number. Game continues until you able to combine tiles. Try to get tile with number 12.

Mode Rings

There are 5 logical ring on game field, each ring marked by its own color. Place figures to game field to fill rings which will be cleared and you get score points. You can undo last move and you can rotate figures. Game continues until you able to place at least one figure. Try to get as much points as you can.

Mode Threes

Touch at any place and slide your finger to any of six directions. All tiles that can be moved will be moved. Tiles can be merged: one with two and vise versa, three with three, six with six and so on. The goal is to get tile with number 6144.

Mode Cube

There are logical lines on the game field, each line goes through two cude sides. Place figures to game field to fill lines which will be cleared and you get score points. You can can rotate figures and undo the last move. The game continues until you able to place at least one figure. Try to get as many points as you can.
